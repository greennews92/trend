Who is Simon Cavill? Simon Cavill is a British actor best known for his roles as Superman in the DC Extended Universe and Geralt of Rivia in the Netflix series The Witcher.

Cavill began his acting career in 2001, with roles in television shows such as The Inspector Lynley Mysteries and Midsomer Murders. He made his film debut in 2002, with a small role in the film The Count of Monte Cristo. Cavill's breakthrough role came in 2011, when he was cast as Superman in the DC Extended Universe. He has since reprised the role in Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (2016), Justice League (2017), and Zack Snyder's Justice League (2021).

In addition to his work in the DC Extended Universe, Cavill has also starred in films such as Stardust (2007), Immortals (2011), and Mission: Impossible Fallout (2018). He currently stars as Geralt of Rivia in the Netflix series The Witcher, which premiered in 2019.

Cavill is a talented actor who has starred in a wide variety of films and television shows. He is best known for his roles as Superman and Geralt of Rivia, but he has also had success in other genres, such as action, adventure, and fantasy.
