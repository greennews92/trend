Who is Juan Manuel Mendoza?

Juan Manuel Mendoza is a Mexican-American artist known for his large-scale public art installations and sculptures.

Mendoza's work often explores themes of identity, immigration, and social justice. His most famous work, the "Border Trilogy," is a series of three sculptures that were installed along the U.S.-Mexico border in 1999.

Mendoza's work has been exhibited in museums and galleries around the world, and he has received numerous awards for his contributions to the art world.
