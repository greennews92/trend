Who is Anna Benson? Anna Benson, born Anna Katarina Benson on December 11, 1975, in Helsingborg, Sweden, is a former model and professional wrestler best known for her time in WWE.

Benson began her career as a model, appearing in magazines such as FHM and Maxim. In 2002, she signed with WWE and was assigned to the SmackDown! brand. She quickly rose through the ranks, becoming one of the most popular Divas on the roster. Benson was known for her beauty, charisma, and in-ring skills. She held the WWE Women's Championship once and was also the first woman to win the WWE Diva Search.

After leaving WWE in 2005, Benson continued to wrestle on the independent circuit. She also appeared in several films and television shows. In 2012, she retired from professional wrestling.

Anna Benson is a pioneer for women in wrestling. She helped to break down barriers and pave the way for other women to succeed in the sport. She is a role model for many young girls and women who aspire to be wrestlers.
