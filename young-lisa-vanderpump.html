Who is the young Lisa Vanderpump?

Lisa Vanderpump is an English businesswoman, television personality, and author. She is best known for her appearances on the reality television shows Vanderpump Rules, The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, and Dancing with the Stars.

Vanderpump was born in London, England, in 1960. She began her career in the hospitality industry at a young age, working as a waitress and bartender. In 1982, she married Ken Todd, and the couple moved to the United States. Together, they opened a number of successful restaurants and bars in Los Angeles, including Villa Blanca, SUR, and Pump.

In 2013, Vanderpump joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. She quickly became a fan favorite, known for her quick wit and sharp tongue. In 2015, she launched her own spin-off show, Vanderpump Rules, which follows the lives of the staff at her restaurant SUR.

Vanderpump is a successful businesswoman and television personality. She is also a philanthropist, and she has donated millions of dollars to various charities.
